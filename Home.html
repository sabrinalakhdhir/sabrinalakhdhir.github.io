About Me

Currently a first-year PhD student at the University of Victoria interested in exploring the concepts of making, digital fabrication, wearables, and customization. I currently have two years of experience conducting human computer interaction research.

From a young age, I've been very involved and interested in all forms of art, and therefore, as a researcher, I am now interested in interdisciplinary projects that fall at the intersection of art and technology.
